Noranda Royalties Inc. is a mining project generator operating across Quebec. We source and structure exploration projects, primarily gold and copper, and bring them to market through options, joint ventures, and direct sales.

On every transaction, we retain a 2% royalty, building an active portfolio of royalty interests alongside our deal flow. Our long-term interests are aligned with the quality of what we bring to market.
